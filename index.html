About Our Project

Our platform is designed to revolutionize the way professionals connect at events and co-working spaces. By providing each user with a unique QR code badge, we eliminate the need for traditional business cards and streamline the networking process.

Key Features:

  • Dynamic, personalized profiles.
  • Effortless sharing of contact information via QR codes.
  • Optional sections to showcase your professional and personal interests.
  • Privacy controls to manage the visibility of your information.
